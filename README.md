## Analyses to explore environmental-dependent effects of APOE genotype on cholesterol, immune, and reproductive outcomes in the Turkana and Orang Asli

### This repository contains scripts to perform analyses presented in Watowich et al. 2026 (bioRxiv).

Divergent Biological Consequences of APOE Isoforms Across Industrialized and Non-Industrial Environments. bioRxiv. 2026
